## Design Direction

### Color Options (final call pending Andrew's gear colors)
- **Option A:** Deep navy + electric blue + gold (premium/elevated)
- **Option B:** Charcoal + lime green or electric orange (high energy/modern)

### Typography
- Headings: Condensed bold sans-serif (Barlow Condensed, Oswald, or Industry)
- Body: Clean sans-serif (Inter or DM Sans)

### UX Principles
- Conversion-first — register CTA above the fold
- Mobile-first
- One clear action per page
- Trust signals (testimonials + action photos) treated as conversion elements
- Hero carries most weight — full-width action photo + headline + CTA

## Copy & Brand Voice

### Tone
Motivational + trustworthy. Sounds like a coach who knows players by name — not a corporate academy. Every line speaks to both the **parent** (trust + value) and the **player** (results + challenge).

### Approved Copy
- **Homepage H1:** "Train Hard. Play Iconic."
- **Subheadline:** "Youth soccer development built for players who want more — coached by someone who has been where you want to go."
- **Primary CTA (sitewide):** Get Started
- **Form submit button:** Send My Message
- **Avoid:** Submit, Click Here, Learn More
