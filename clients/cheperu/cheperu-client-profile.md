## Change Requests

### Specials Section — Availability Disclaimer
**Requested by:** Pracilla  
**Date logged:** 2026-04-10  
**Status:** Copy drafted — pending implementation

**Context:** Specials are not always available. Products sell out, so Pracilla wants customers to ask their server about availability before ordering. This should be reflected both at the section level and within individual item descriptions.

**Approved copy (drafted by Aurther):**

**Section-level disclaimer** (top of Specials section):
> Our specials change with what's freshest. Please check with your server before ordering the specials — some items may not be available.

**Item-level note** (under individual special descriptions):
> Ask your server — availability varies daily.

**Implementation notes:**
- Item-level note can be applied selectively to items most likely to sell out
- Consider styling it in italics or lighter text so it reads as a helpful aside, not a warning
